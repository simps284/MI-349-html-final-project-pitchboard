Title

Personal Portfolio: Joe Simpson

Short Pitch

This website will be a way for me to showcase my skills to potential employers

Long Pitch

Game development is a complicated field with many different disciplines coming together to make a product. Because of this, it is important to find a role to specialize in and demonstrate that you have the skills to fulfill that role. The purpose of this portfolio website will be to showcase my work in the game development program at MSU to show potential employers that I have the potential to contribute as a designer on any game dev team. This way employers will know not only what I aim to do at the company, but that I have the skills to do it.

Personas

  • Employer: Someone who may view this alongside my resume when looking for an internship or job
  • Professor: Someone who may view this for certain classes that require a portfolio as a prereq.
  • Friends/Family: People who to show this to when they ask "what exactly is it that you do for a living"
